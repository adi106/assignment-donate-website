Our Donate Plan

We want to help you avoid wasting time and money on projects or short-term
teams that don't have a lasting impact, and end up hurting while you are trying to
help. Your group can play a vital part in addressing extreme poverty and unnecessary suffering.

Food for a family

For communities around the world, facing hunger affects their livelihood. 720+ million people across the world don’t receive enough nutrition to lead an active, healthy life, or provide for themselves and their families. We try our best to help those family with donations.

Shelter for a family

With over 20 years of experience, we are experts in emergency shelter after disaster. We often work in places that others don’t, making sure that we reach families who need support to take the next step in resuming their livelihoods.

Education for a child

The best way to change a child’s life is to change the world they live in. When you sponsor a child, you are not only helping them, but also you are changing an entire community to ensure sustainability and giving children the best opportunities for a stable and fruitful life.
